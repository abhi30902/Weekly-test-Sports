Racket (or Racquet): Badminton rackets are used by players to hit the shuttlecock over the net. They come in various styles and weights to suit different playing styles.

Shuttlecock (or Birdie): A shuttlecock is the projectile used in badminton. It has a feather or synthetic skirt and a cork base. There are two types of shuttlecocks: feathered and plastic.

Net: The badminton net is used to divide the court into two sides and is set at a specific height.

Court: The badminton court is marked with lines for singles and doubles play. It includes boundary lines, a centerline, and service boxes.

Court Shoes: Specialized badminton shoes with non-marking soles provide grip and stability on the court.
